1. Total Quantity by Category
   - Electronics shows strong sales demand.
   - Stationery has high volume due to bulk purchases.
   - Furniture has lower transaction frequency.

   Insight:
   High-quantity categories may require stronger
   inventory management and supplier planning.


2. High Sales Volume Categories
   - Stationery qualifies due to bulk buying behavior.

   Insight:
   Bulk-order categories can be targeted for
   wholesale pricing or promotional strategies.


3. Average Order Value by Category
   - Electronics has higher average order value.
   - Stationery and Furniture show lower spending per order.

   Insight:
   Electronics drives revenue even with fewer orders.


4. Low Average Price Categories
   - Stationery and Furniture fall below ₹1000 average.

   Insight:
   Opportunity exists for upselling or bundled offers.


5. Order Value Classification
   - Helps identify premium vs regular customers.

   Insight:
   High-value customers can be targeted for loyalty
   programs or personalized marketing.
